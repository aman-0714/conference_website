The International Conference on Intelligent Power, Energy and Sustainable Systems (IIPESS-2027) is a premier event that brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to discuss the latest advancements in the fields of power systems, renewable energy, signal processing techniques, and artificial intelligence. The conference provides a platform for sharing innovative research, fostering collaborations, and exploring emerging trends in these interdisciplinary domains.

IIPESS-2027 will be hosted by Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar, bringing together a global community of innovators and thought leaders in power and energy systems. The conference is to be an IEEE technically co-sponsored event (with approval under process) and is tentatively scheduled for 26th – 28th March 2027. The conference aims to provide a vibrant platform for academicians, researchers, industry professionals, and students to exchange ideas and showcase cutting-edge advancements in the domains of power systems, power electronics and drives, signal processing, and AI/ML. IIPESS-2027 is designed to foster collaboration, innovation, and knowledge exchange, addressing contemporary challenges in energy systems. It will serve as a catalyst for discussing developments in next-generation intelligent and sustainable energy solutions, supporting the global vision of a reliable, secure, and low-carbon energy future.

About NIT Jalandhar

Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar is among the 31 NITs established by Ministry of Education (earlier MHRD), Govt. of India. The Institute came into existence in the year 1987 (earlier Regional Engineering College, Jalandhar) and obtained the status "Institute of National Importance" by Act of Parliament 2007. The institute is offering B.Tech., M.Sc., M.Tech., and Ph.D. programmes in various disciplines. It includes Electrical Engineering, Instrumentation and Control Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Biotechnology, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Industrial and Production Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Textile Technology, etc.NIT Jalandhar achieved an impressive 46th rank in the NIRF Engineering Rankings 2023, reflecting its strong academic and research excellence.

Department of Electrical Engineering

The Department of Electrical Engineering was started in the year 2013. The department presently offers undergraduate programme B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering (EE), M.Tech in Electric Vehicle Design (EVD) and research programme Ph.D. in various specialization of Electrical Engineering and allied areas. The Department aims to impart high quality education to the students and carry out fundamental and industry-oriented research work. The major research specialization of the department are Power System, Energy System, Emerging Areas, and Control System. The department has highly qualified and competent faculty members and adequate facilities to support teaching and learning activities. The alumni of Department are well placed in the top echelons of industry & academia both in India & abroad.
