Author Guidelines – IIPESS-2027

Authors are requested to carefully read and follow all guidelines before submitting their manuscript. Papers not adhering to these guidelines may be desk-rejected without review.

📝 Paper Format

All manuscripts must be prepared using the IEEE Conference Paper Template. Papers should be in A4 size, two-column format, using 10pt Times New Roman font. The template is available on the IEEE website.

📏 Paper Length

Regular papers: 4 to 6 pages including figures, tables, and references. Extended papers up to 8 pages may be accepted with prior approval and additional charges.

🔍 Plagiarism Policy

IIPESS-2027 strictly follows IEEE plagiarism policies. Similarity index must be below 30% (including References). Papers exceeding this limit will be summarily rejected. Authors must obtain permission for any third-party copyrighted content.

🌐 Submission Language

All submissions must be in English. Authors are advised to proofread carefully for grammar, spelling, and technical accuracy before submission.

👥 Authorship

All listed authors must have made a genuine contribution to the work. The corresponding author is responsible for ensuring accuracy of all author information at the time of submission.

📁 File Format

Submit papers in PDF format only. The file size should not exceed 5 MB. Ensure all fonts are embedded in the PDF. Scanned or image-based PDFs will not be accepted.

🔄 Revision Policy

Authors of papers requiring minor/major revisions will be notified after peer review. Revised manuscripts must be resubmitted within the stipulated deadline along with a point-by-point response to reviewers.

📋 Registration Requirement

At least one author per accepted paper must register for the conference and present the work. Papers without a registered presenter will be removed from the proceedings.

Important: Detailed submission instructions and the IEEE template link will be updated on the paper submission page. For queries, contact the organizing secretariat.
