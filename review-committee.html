Review Committee – IIPESS-2027

All papers submitted to IIPESS-2027 undergo a rigorous double-blind peer review process coordinated by the Technical Program Chairs, supported by subject-matter reviewers drawn from academia and industry across the conference's eight tracks. Reviewers evaluate submissions for technical merit, originality, and relevance to the conference theme, Intelligence Powering Sustainability.
