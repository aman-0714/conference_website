Conference Tracks – IIPESS-2027

IIPESS-2027 invites original research contributions across the following tracks. The conference covers a broad and multidisciplinary scope spanning Power Systems, Power Electronics, Renewable Energy, Smart Grids, Electric Vehicles, Signal Processing, High Voltage Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence.

Track 1

Power System Protection, Operation, Control, and Security

Track 2

Advanced Power Electronics and Drives

Track 3

Energy Storage Technologies, Electric Vehicles, and Future Transportation

Track 4

Advancements in Smart Grids, Microgrids, and Nano-grids

Track 5

Signal Processing and Instrumentation for Power and Energy Systems

Track 6

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data-driven Applications for Power and Energy Systems

Track 7

Recent Developments in High Voltage Engineering

Track 8

Sustainable and Renewable Energy Technologies

Note: Complete track details will be updated soon.

IEEE follows stringent policies for plagiarism. Papers having similarities beyond a specified limit of 30% (with References) will be straightaway rejected and therefore, it should be below 30% for camera-ready papers. The authors also need to obtain permission from the copyright holder for third-party content use, if any.
