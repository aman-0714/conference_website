About IEEE

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is a leading global professional association dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity, with a long-standing presence in the fields of electrical engineering, electronics, computing, and allied disciplines. It supports a wide network of conferences, journals, and standards that serve researchers, practitioners, and students worldwide.

IIPESS-2027 is planned as an IEEE technically co-sponsored event, with approval currently under process. Technical co-sponsorship status will be confirmed and updated on this page once approval is received.

IEEE technical co-sponsorship, where granted, typically signifies that a conference's technical content and review process meet IEEE's quality standards, and may make accepted, presented papers eligible for submission to IEEE Xplore for indexing, subject to IEEE's own review and final approval.

For the most current and authoritative information about IEEE itself, please visit ieee.org.
