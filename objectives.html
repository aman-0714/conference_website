Objectives – IIPESS-2027

The International Conference on Intelligent Power, Energy and Sustainable Systems (IIPESS-2027) is designed to serve as a premier forum for the exchange of ideas, research findings, and innovations in the rapidly evolving fields of power systems, energy technologies, and artificial intelligence. The key objectives of the conference are:

🔬 Research Exchange

Provide a vibrant platform for academicians, researchers, and students to present and discuss cutting-edge research in power systems, power electronics, and AI/ML.

🤝 Industry–Academia Collaboration

Foster meaningful collaboration between industry professionals and academic researchers to address real-world challenges in energy and power systems.

🌱 Sustainable Energy Future

Serve as a catalyst for discussions on next-generation intelligent and sustainable energy solutions supporting a reliable, secure, and low-carbon energy future.

💡 Innovation and Knowledge Transfer

Encourage sharing of innovative ideas, emerging technologies, and best practices across interdisciplinary domains including smart grids, EVs, and renewable energy.

🌐 Global Networking

Bring together a global community of thought leaders, engineers, and researchers to build collaborative networks for future research and development.

📄 Quality Publication

Provide a high-quality peer-reviewed publication platform for accepted papers, with IEEE technical co-sponsorship (subject to approval), ensuring wide visibility and impact.
