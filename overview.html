Overview – IIPESS-2027

IIPESS-2027 will be hosted by Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar, bringing together a global community of innovators and thought leaders in power and energy systems. The conference is to be an IEEE technically co-sponsored event (with approval under process) and is tentatively scheduled for 26th – 28th March 2027. The conference aims to provide a vibrant platform for academicians, researchers, industry professionals, and students to exchange ideas and showcase cutting-edge advancements in the domains of power systems, power electronics and drives, signal processing, and AI/ML. IIPESS-2027 is designed to foster collaboration, innovation, and knowledge exchange, addressing contemporary challenges in energy systems. It will serve as a catalyst for discussing developments in next-generation intelligent and sustainable energy solutions, supporting the global vision of a reliable, secure, and low-carbon energy future.
