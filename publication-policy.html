Publication Policy – IIPESS-2027

IIPESS-2027 is to be an IEEE technically co-sponsored event (approval under process) and follows IEEE's publication and ethics standards for all accepted papers.

🔍 Plagiarism & Similarity Policy

The conference follows stringent anti-plagiarism policies as laid down by IEEE. Papers with similarity exceeding 30% (including References) will be straightaway rejected. Camera-ready papers must also be below the 30% threshold prior to final acceptance.

©️ Copyright & Third-Party Content

Authors must obtain prior permission from the copyright holder for any use of third-party content (figures, tables, data, or text) included in their manuscript, and must furnish proof of permission if requested during the review or proceedings preparation process.

📚 Proceedings & Indexing

Accepted and presented papers, subject to IEEE technical co-sponsorship approval, are intended for submission to IEEE Xplore for indexing. Final indexing remains subject to IEEE's quality review and approval process.

📝 Originality

Submitted manuscripts must be original, unpublished work that is not under review or consideration for publication elsewhere at the time of submission to IIPESS-2027.

🎙️ Presentation Requirement

At least one author of every accepted paper must register for the conference and present the paper in person or virtually (for hybrid participation) for it to be considered for inclusion in the final proceedings.

⚖️ Ethics

Authors are expected to adhere to IEEE's publication ethics, including proper attribution, disclosure of conflicts of interest, and accurate reporting of research findings.

Note: Complete publication and indexing details will be updated as IEEE technical co-sponsorship approval progresses. For specific queries, contact the Organizing Secretariat via the Contact page.
