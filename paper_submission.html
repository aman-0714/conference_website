⚠ Page length should not exceed 6 pages (IEEE format).

Plagiarism Policy: The conference follows stringent policies for plagiarism as laid by IEEE. Papers having similarities beyond a specified limit of 30% (with References) will be straightaway rejected. Similarity must be below 30% for camera-ready papers. Authors also need to obtain permission from the copyright holder for any third-party content use.
